A fitness bar is a food that is designed to increase physical energy. Usually, fitness bars contain a combination of proteins - fats - carbohydrates, in addition, they are enriched with vitamins and minerals.
The role of vitamins and minerals is not only to help achieve higher results. They are also important for your body to be able to withstand heavy loads.
Gainer is a specialized sports food mixture containing a certain ratio of protein and carbohydrates. Depending on the purpose and type of training, gainers are used as a complete sports nutrition or as a supplement.
Proteins are a key nutritional supplement for every active athlete. Proteins help in building muscle mass and at the same time provide protection and regeneration of muscle tissue that wears out during training.
